❤️  Colors Declare 
RGB, HEX, HSL, RGBA, HSLA values.

rgb(255, 99, 71)
rgba(200, 255, 71, 0.5)
rgba(red, green, blue, alpha) : alpha is color opacity....

hsl(hue, saturation, lightness)
hsl(9, 100%, 64%)
hsla(hue, saturation, lightness, alpha)

Hexadecimal : 6 or 3 digit
#000000 , #ffffff
